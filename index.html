Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to our users. The types of information we collect depend on how you use our app.

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and other profile information.
  • Content: We collect the content you create, upload, or receive from others when using our services.
  • Payment Information: If you make purchases, we collect payment information, but we don't store full credit card details.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you use our app, including features you use and time spent.
  • Device Information: We collect device-specific information like hardware model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
  • Log Information: When you use our services, we automatically collect and store certain information in server logs.

Note: We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide, maintain, and improve our services, develop new ones, and protect Meta Dev App and our users.

Specific Uses Include:

  • Providing and personalizing our services
  • Developing new features and services
  • Measuring performance and analyzing usage
  • Communicating with you about our services
  • Protecting against fraud and abuse
  • Legal compliance

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share personal information with companies, organizations, or individuals outside of Meta except in the following cases:

With Your Consent

We will share personal information with third parties when we have your consent to do so.

For Legal Reasons

We will share personal information if we believe that access, use, preservation, or disclosure of the information is reasonably necessary to:

  • Meet any applicable law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request
  • Enforce applicable Terms of Service, including investigation of potential violations
  • Detect, prevent, or otherwise address fraud, security, or technical issues
  • Protect against harm to the rights, property or safety of our users, the public, or Meta

Data Security

We work hard to protect our users from unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information we hold.

Security Measures Include:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing
  • Access controls to limit access to personal information
  • Security training for our employees

Remember: While we strive to protect your information, no security system is impenetrable. We cannot guarantee the security of our databases.

Your Rights and Choices

You have choices regarding the information we collect and how it's used.

Access and Correction

You can access and update most of your personal information through your account settings.

Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations we may have.

Communication Preferences

You can choose whether to receive promotional communications from us via email, SMS, or push notifications.

Location Information

You can control whether we collect location information through your device settings.

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to provide and improve our services.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the app to function properly
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our app
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads

Managing Cookies

Most browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may impact your experience with our services.

International Data Transfers

We may transfer your information outside of your country of residence for the purposes described in this policy.

When we transfer information internationally, we implement safeguards to ensure your data receives an adequate level of protection.

For transfers from the European Economic Area (EEA) to countries not deemed adequate by the European Commission, we've put in place appropriate measures to protect your personal data.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16.

If we become aware that a child under 16 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

If you believe a child under 16 may have provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may change this privacy policy from time to time. We will post any changes on this page and, if the changes are significant, we will provide a more prominent notice.

We will also keep prior versions of this privacy policy in an archive for your review.

We encourage you to review our privacy policy whenever you use our services to stay informed about our information practices and the ways you can help protect your privacy.
